Account.java

Represents a customer's bank account.

Fields

id
Primary key.

accountNumber
Unique account identifier.

accountName
Display name of the account.

balance
Money currently in the account.

accountType
Savings or Current.

customer
Owner of the account.

createdAt
Date the account was opened.


AccountType

Stores available account types.

CURRENT

SAVINGS


Repository

findByAccountNumber()

Returns one account.

existsByAccountNumber()

Checks duplicate account numbers.

findByCustomer()

Returns every account owned by a customer.


DTOs

CreateAccountRequest

Used when opening an account.

UpdateAccountRequest

Allows changing account name.

AccountResponse

Returned back to Swagger/Postman.


Mapper

Converts Account entity into AccountResponse.

Never expose entities directly.
